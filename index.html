Voice of Global South
Global South Centre of Excellence is the culmination of India’s vision to have a formalized institution. The idea for this mechanism of mobilizing nations from the Global South was articulated in January 2023 by Hon'ble Prime Minister of India Shri Narendra Modi. Established as a part of Research and Information System for Developing Countries (RIS), GSCE endeavors to assist relevant stakeholders and partners in the identification and implementation of scalable and sustainable solutions that can transform the economies and societies of Global South countries.
